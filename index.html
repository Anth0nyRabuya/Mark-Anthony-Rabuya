Get to know us

Eko kalikasan Hub takes peaceful action to confront decision-makers and hold them accountable to people and the planet. We work with a diversity of communities in Malaysia to promote peace, to develop effective solutions to environmental problems, and to inspire others to create positive change in their own lives.

Eco Kalikasan Hub’s mission is to ensure the Earth’s ability to nurture life in all its diversity. To achieve this, we focus on protecting biodiversity in all its forms, preventing pollution and the abuse of the Earth’s oceans, land, air, and fresh water, ending all nuclear threats, and promoting peace, global disarmament, and nonviolence.

To volunteer with us, simply sign up on our website, where you can explore a variety of opportunities to get involved. Whether you’re interested in grassroots campaigning, digital activism, or joining local events, there’s a role for you. Our volunteers are essential in spreading awareness, participating in environmental actions, and helping us create meaningful change. No matter your skills or experience, you can be part of our movement to protect the planet.

At Eco Kalikasan Hub, we are tackling critical issues like climate justice, fighting against air pollution, and advocating for a plastic-free future. With the help of communities, we work to protect biodiversity, prevent environmental abuse, and hold corporations accountable for their impact on our planet. Together, we aim to create a sustainable and just world for everyone.
