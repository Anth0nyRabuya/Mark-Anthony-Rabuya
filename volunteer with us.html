Become a Eco Kalikasan Hub volunteer!

Eco Kalikasan Hub volunteers are vital to the organisation as they bring global campaigns to the whole of society through information, awareness-raising and citizen mobilisation.

There are many ways to volunteer with Eco Kaliksan Hub to protect the planet (and have fun, too!).

Types of environmental volunteering at Eco Kalikasan Hub

Tree Planting and Reforestation

Propose a reforestation program to your municipal council. Align the project with the municipality's climate action or environmental plans.

Engage with Local Government
Coastal and Marine Conservation

Work with coastal barangays to organize marine conservation efforts, such as coral restoration or mangrove replanting.

Collaborate with Fisherfolk and Coastal Communities
Community Clean-Up Drives

Set up collection points for recyclable materials in partnership with waste management companies.

Organize Campaigns on Waste Segregation and Recycling
Raise Awareness in society

Raise awareness and inform people about the solutions that exist to address environmental problems at talks, tents or in schools.
